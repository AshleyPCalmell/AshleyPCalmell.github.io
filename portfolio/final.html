    This project from 2021 was focused around replicating the articles we are given when looking up "Top 10 most attractive people" but with a twist. The images made for the subject in this article were made through Runway's Spade Coco, which took the subjects and made them a pallet. With them being scrambled into this unbiased image of "attractiveness", I played with that concept and made the articles made from "robots". Robots would be an unbiased interpretation of beauty, and this is shown through the entire article, including the ads. This is suppose to be comical but also ironic towards how much care people put into beauty standards.
