    • The Self

    This photography series was made in 2022, and is centered around objects that represent me personally. Many of these photos were taking with the idea of portraiture, but some ended up being more like snap shots in time. Skating has become a large part of my life since quarantine, and crystals have been a hobby I have fallen into as-well. I want my works in this series to show a more practical life of a young adult in 2022, so many of the topics I chose are things my friends and peers also involve themselves in. The main feeling I wish to evoke is a sense of comfort to the viewer, and this series is utilized to reveal what comfort me specifically.
