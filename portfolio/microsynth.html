    • Walk In San Jose

    This was a project created in 2023 where I soldered and programed a motherboard inside of a box to play a 8bit song from a game called Undertale. I programed buttons, levers, and a speaker to all interact, allowing the user to speed up the music, turn up the volume, change the melody, or turn the song off. There are also LEDs that play alongside the music, similar to a music box. I also designed the box based off the game that the song correlates to. The code was done with Arduino.
