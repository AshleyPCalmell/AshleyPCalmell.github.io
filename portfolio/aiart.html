Runway (AdaIN-Style) Art

These photos are a series of mashups

The program used to make these photos is from AdaIN-style from Runway. The Ai takes two photos and mashes them together to create a whole new work. I decided to combine photos of baby animals and add a texture of the popularized food they are eaten as. This series is used to highlight the innocence of the animals that are brutally killed to make people "favorite" foods. (bacon, meat, fried chicken)
