    Final 3D print:

    This 3D printed artwork was made in 2021. I wanted to make a 3D print of an object I loved as a child so I could use it as my own statue to keep forever. I decided to use Master Chief's helmet, a character from a video game I played a lot as a child with my brothers. I painted one of the works and gifted it to my brother while the other stays with me, sitting above my desk. It was made in the spirit of remembering those good times with brother during our youth.
