  My art serves as a portal to the vivid imagination and imagery that defined the digital generation.
  Growing up alongside the internet, I am deeply inspired by the playful and captivating aesthetics of 
  children's cartoons and video games from the 2000s. In my work, I fuse this nostalgia with cutting-edge
  software and interactive elements to create a dynamic bridge between past and present.

  My creative process is a tapestry woven from the threads of my childhood experiences and the simple joys
  that once captivated me. From a young age, I explored traditional art forms such as painting, drawing, and
  photography, which laid the foundation for my artistic journey. As I transitioned into the digital realm,
  I made a conscious effort to maintain a connection to my roots and youthful wonder.

  I revel in experimenting with a diverse array of software and mediums, allowing me to embark on a range of 
  creative projects. With each new endeavor, I aim to push the boundaries of what each software can achieve,
  constantly expanding my understanding of their capabilities.

  My intention is to transport viewers back to a simpler time, inviting them to see the world through
  the nostalgic lens of someone who came of age in the 2000s. I seek to evoke emotion, spark conversations,
  and encourage interaction with every piece. Through my art, I hope to kindle a sense of shared enthusiasm 
  for the ever-evolving and morphing technologies that have shaped our lives.

  Thank you for joining me in celebrating the magic and wonder of the technology that has been 
  woven into the fabric of our generation.

    Artist CV
